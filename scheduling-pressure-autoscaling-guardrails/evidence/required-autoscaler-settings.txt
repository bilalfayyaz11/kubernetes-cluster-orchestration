Required Cluster Autoscaler settings:

--expander=least-waste
--scale-down-utilization-threshold=0.5
--scale-down-unneeded-time=2m
--scale-down-delay-after-add=2m
--skip-nodes-with-system-pods=false
--skip-nodes-with-local-storage=false
--v=4

Infrastructure requirement:

The controller must also receive a real cloud-provider configuration and
node-group discovery configuration.

Valid examples include:

AWS:
--cloud-provider=aws
--node-group-auto-discovery=asg:tag=k8s.io/cluster-autoscaler/enabled,kubernetes.io/cluster/<cluster-name>

Cluster API:
--cloud-provider=clusterapi
--node-group-auto-discovery=clusterapi:namespace=<namespace>,clusterName=<cluster-name>

The existing kind workers are not members of either an AWS Auto Scaling Group
or a Cluster API MachineDeployment, MachineSet, or MachinePool.
